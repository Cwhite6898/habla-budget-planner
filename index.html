ℹ️ Assumptions & notes ▶

Revenue assumptions

This planner uses more conservative revenue targets than the budget approved in August 2025. Gala ($51,400 vs. $61,400), Donation Drive ($7,000 vs. $20,000), Buy-a-spot ($5,000 vs. $12,000), and Merchandise ($1,000 vs. $4,000) — about $31,000 less in total. Use the revenue controls to explore hitting the approved targets.

One-time FY2026 expenses not included

ADTLE teacher conference, Theater program subsidy, and Field trips have been removed as one-time investments. Middle school learning opportunities reduced from $9,624 to $4,716 for the same reason.

Not included in this model

Yearbook — break-even. Income ($7,000) and expense ($7,001) net to ~$0.

Baile de los Muertos — community event, break-even by design. Income and cost both $4,000.

RG Food Drive — a restricted fund with donations that can only be used for the food drive.

How this works

Dropping a fundraiser removes its revenue and its cost. All figures are cash basis.

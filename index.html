FY2027 (Jul 2026 – Jun 2027)  ·  Cash basis  ·  For internal planning use

⚠︎ Note on revenue assumptions: This planner uses more conservative revenue targets than the budget approved in August 2025. Gala ($51,400 vs. $61,400 approved), Donation Drive ($7,000 vs. $20,000), Buy-a-spot ($5,000 vs. $12,000), and Merchandise ($1,000 vs. $4,000) are all set lower — about $31,000 less in total. That's why the starting position shows a deficit. Use the fundraiser sliders to explore what happens if we hit the August-approved targets.
ⓘ︎ One-time FY2026 expenses not included: ADTLE teacher conference, Theater program subsidy, and Field trips have been removed as they were one-time investments not expected to recur. Middle school learning opportunities has been reduced from $9,624 to $4,716 for the same reason.
